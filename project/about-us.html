A PROUD IRISH ROOTS STORY

Better with Dew Valley

Starting in Thurles, we are proud to be providing premium cooked bacon for over thirty years, supplying exceptional streaky bacon from our purpose-built facilty where natural curing meets cutting-edge technique.

SMOKING TECHNOLOGY

Traditional smoke, modern control

Our smoking systems give you that familiar, authentic bacon character while tightly controlling consistancy and quality. By carefully managing smoke time, wood selection and airflow, we can deliver profiles ranging from subtle and sweet to bold and robust.

Working closely with customers, we tune smoke levels to suit diffrent applications – from retail packs and food-to-go sandwiches through to ready meals, pizzas and hot food counters.

  • Natural hardwood smoke options
  • Controlled, repeatable smoke intensity
  • Support for cleaner labels and product claims
  • Consistant colour and aroma across batches
