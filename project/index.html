We are a privately owned independent company with our Head Office and EU-approved ultra-modern factory in Thurles, Co. Tipperary.

Dew Valley was the first company in Europe to introduce the benefits of microwave technology to the commercial production of cooked bacon. We are European leaders in microwave technology, enabling us to supply a wide range of exceptionally consistent products. We use only the finest raw materials from Ireland, the U.K., and selected EU sources.

Our ready-cooked streaky rashers already enjoy a significant share of the U.K. prepared bacon sandwich and burger market and we have diversified into value added products to satisfy the requirements of our customers.
